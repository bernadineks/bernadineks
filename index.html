Pareidolia

To be thrown into the confinement of our conciseness drives us to a corner. A place where we hallucinate everything we see through these eyes. Inspite of it being a reality we want, it’s only a dream we can live in. That’s how strong the confinement of consciousness is. This begins the Paraedolia, an escape from this world. To see things as our mind pleases. To see, to feel, to know things as our thought pleases.
