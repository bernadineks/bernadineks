Pareidolia

When thrown into confinement of our self consciousness, we drive to an unfathomable corner, where we hallucinate everything. Everything we see through changes, our eyes changes. Inspite of it being a reality, we cannot live in it because it's a mere dream. But still, the strong confinement of consciousness doesn't let go easily. And that's what Pareidolia is; an escape from this world. To see things as our mind pleases, to feel as our body craves, and yet in constant urge to escape.
